About Us

Little information

Being informed is critical in deciding your medical choice of treatment. And we make sure that you know about your diagnosis and choices for treatment options by providing the expert second opinion on your medical problems. We are specialized in reviewing your current treatment plan and come up with the comprehensive conclusions. Our specialist doctors from the expert panel study your case and make you aware about your options. Currently we are operating in Pune only. We will be coming to your city shortly if you are not from Pune.




When to Ask for Second Opinion -

  •  Before having surgery


  •  New diagnosis


  •  Costly treatment


  •  Change in medication


  •  Planning treatments after surgery


  •  Whenever you think it is going to affect your health


Our Ethics

  • We will keep your medical records confidential.


  • MCC is qualified medical person, generally a doctor having BHMS/BAMS/MBBS degree.


  • Panel doctors who will actually be giving the second opinion are specialists having MD/MS/DNB or above.


  • We strongly discourage taking the treatment from our panel doctors to help us delivering a genuine second opinion.


Benefits

What are the benefits for you?

Experienced medical assistant

You will get the experienced medical assistant (MCC) to manage your case end to end. His experience is invaluable in revealing the things which are not possible for non-medico person otherwise. He will help you out in formulating your queries as well.

Key of our service

The most important benefit is that you will get the opinions from two or more specialists with varied expertise. This is the key of our service that case will be critically analyzed if more than one experts are involved. And the important fact is these specialists will study the case independently and they will not be knowing each other.

No treatment

We don't provide the treatment strictly, so that there is no conflict of interest and you will not get wrong or biased opinion.

No hidden cost

If your medical condition requires opinions from more than two specialists, you will not be charged extra. No hidden costs.

Price range

Most of the specialities will be covered between the price range of Rs. 800 to Rs 2000.

How It Works

Work Flow

You can reach us on xxxxx xxxxx or drop an email to initiate the process.


Medical Case Coordinator(MCC) will be assigned to your case who will take care of your complete case from start to end. MCC is the medico person who will assist you to uncover the valuable information about your condition.


MCC will make arrangements to get all your medical records in place and put those in a suitable format so that it can be presented to the expert consultants.


Opiniya will select the best specialists from the panel, after studying your medical requirements (specialty, diagnosis, your queries). Opiniya will choose at least two specialists or more, depending on the case to get the opinion on your case.


MCC will forward your documents and your queries to selected specialists. MCC will keep you updated on the status of your case.


Specialist will review your case and will coordinate with MCC to know anything necessary about your case.


After thorough analysis and comprehensive review of your case, specialist will share their opinion with MCC who in turn will go through all the opinions and form the conclusion.


MCC will provide these opinions and conclusion to you and make sure your all doubts are answered carefully.


And now you are ready with your options to take your treatment to the further level. With no confusion and only certainty.
