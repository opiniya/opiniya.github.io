How It Works

Work Flow

You can reach us on xxxxx xxxxx or drop an email to initiate the process.


Medical Case Coordinator(MCC) will be assigned to your case who will take care of your complete case from start to end. MCC is the medico person who will assist you to uncover the valuable information about your condition.


MCC will make arrangements to get all your medical records in place and put those in a suitable format so that it can be presented to the expert consultants.


Opiniya will select the best specialists from the panel, after studying your medical requirements (specialty, diagnosis, your queries). Opiniya will choose at least two specialists or more, depending on the case to get the opinion on your case.


MCC will forward your documents and your queries to selected specialists. MCC will keep you updated on the status of your case.


Specialist will review your case and will coordinate with MCC to know anything necessary about your case.


After thorough analysis and comprehensive review of your case, specialist will share their opinion with MCC who in turn will go through all the opinions and form the conclusion.


MCC will provide these opinions and conclusion to you and make sure your all doubts are answered carefully.


And now you are ready with your options to take your treatment to the further level. With no confusion and only certainty.
